What Your Results Mean?

This is a proof of concept project. My goal is to prove that deep learning algorithms can be used to detect mental health issues.

At this stage, the results given are not conclusive. I am continously working improve the project and if you think you could help, contact me on my email id: yash2998chhabria@gmail.com. If you are a developer feel free to see the code or Fork/Clone the project from the github links provided below.

Currently, There are two Deep learning algorithms at work. The first one is used to detect over the overall sentiment of the article (96% accuracy). The Second one (Sentence wise : 76% accuracy) detects symptoms mentioned on National Health Service (Uk) Website.

Algorithms At Work!?


Overall Depression Sentiment Detection

A Bilateral Mutilayer Recurrent Neural Network trained on data scraped from time-to-change.org.uk and beyondblue.org


Sentence Wise Detection of Symptoms of Depression

A Bilateral Mutilayer Recurrent Neural Network trained on data scraped from the reddit subreddit r/depressed
